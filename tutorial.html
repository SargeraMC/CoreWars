CoreWars Tutorial

#1 Introduction

To get started, you'll want to run off from spawn and find a safe place. Once you've found a safe place, you can place your generator down. Your generator is your source of gold. You can see how much gold you have on your sidebar.

Be careful, though, people can fight you and break your generator if they find you!

#2 Generators

Generators are your source of income. To start producing gold, place down your generator and it will produce gold every 8 seconds. To withdraw this gold, you can either shift-click on it, or open the GUI by right clicking on the generator. Here you will also find various upgrade options such as:

  • Level: How much gold is produced by your generator every 8 seconds
  • Storage: How much gold your generator can store before it stops producing.

    • #3 Supply Crates

    Around the map, you will find shulker boxes of different colors. The items you find in these shulker boxes will depend on the color of the shulker. The supply crates will contain useful items such as:

  • Combat Items: Anything from wooden to diamond swords/armor can be found.
  • Defensive Blocks: These Blocks can be used in building your base.
  • Generators: You can find extra generators with different storage/level values too!

    • #4 Base Building

    Once you've found some blocks, you should build your base. This will make a safe place for you to place your generators and produce gold in peace. There are different tiers of blocks, which will have different health and therefore will take different damage from TNT depending on the tier.

    To build a base, you will first need to place a core. These can be found around the map in shulkers.

  • These blocks, in order, are: Stripped Oak Wood, Polished Diorite, Polished Andesite & Smooth Slab.
  • There are also door varieties of these blocks, including: Oak Door, Spruce Door & Iron Doors.
    • #5 Combat

    From killing other players, you will get gold and experience. Be careful though, if you kill the same player too much in a short space of time, you will trigger the anti-kil farming check.

  • Note: Combat Items (e.g. sword, armour) are lost on death. All other items are kept.

    • #6 Prestige | $100 Prestige Top Reward

    The end objective of this server is to achieve the highest rank of prestige that you can. Whoever achieves the highest prestige by the end of the season will get the $100 PayPal reward. Prestiging will cost you money and will have a level requirement. However, in return you will receive a 0.2x booster increase (this will scale with donation boosters).
